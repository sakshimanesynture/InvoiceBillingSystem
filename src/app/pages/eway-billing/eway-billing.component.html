What is e-Way Bill Generation Software

e-Way bill generation software or e-way bill software is a software or a feature of software designed to help businesses generate e-way bills directly from their billing or invoicing software. With the e-way bill software in place, businesses don't have to visit the official e-way Bill portal to generate e-way bills. They can effortlessly do it from the billing software.

Note: e-Way bill generation was made mandatory for transporting goods exceeding â‚¹50,000. According to the regulation, the person carrying the consignment must carry an e-Way bill related to the supply. However, only registered businesses, either the supplier, recipient or the transporter of the goods, can generate an e-way bill.
