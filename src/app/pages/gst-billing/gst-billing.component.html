E-Invoicing Software?

E-invoice software or e-invoicing software is a feature offered by billing or invoicing software that allows businesses to generate e-invoice directly from the software without visiting the official e-invoice portal.

Ever since e-invoicing was mandated for all B2B transactions, the government has been suggesting businesses either upgrade their existing billing software to support the e-invoicing functionality or have the software that provides the e-invoicing feature. This suggests the seriousness of having reliable e-invoice software.

Whether you're a small to medium business or a large enterprise, if your annual turnover is more than INR 5 Crore, you must generate e-invoices for all your B2B transactions.

Relying on a government e-invoice portal to generate e-invoice may not be ideal if your business handles multiple B2B transactions on a daily basis. An e-invoicing software makes your job easy by enabling you to generate invoices directly from the comfort of your business.

Why Choose Bill e-Invoicing Software

Instant Generation of e-Invoices

No more dealing with the busy e-invoice official portal to generate your e-invoices. With Bill, you can generate e-invoices instantly in a single click.

Once you enable the e-invoicing feature on Bill software, you can start generating e-invoices for all eligible transactions. As per GST norms, you can currently generate e-invoices for B2B sales invoices, debit notes, and credit notes only.

Bulk e-Invoice Generation

If you have multiple invoices to generate e-invoices, you can push all of them at the same time for validation to the IRP.

This feature helps you to reduce manual effort significantly and saves time. You can check the status of each e-invoice individually from the detailed dashboard.

If any of the invoices fail for generation, you can pick them, add the required data and push for validation again.

Generate e-Way Bills Along With e-Invoices

While generating e-invoices, if any of your invoices require e-way bill, you can generate the same at the time of e-invoice generation.

Bill e-invoice software provides two options while generating e-invoices:

  • Generate e-Invoice - For invoices without transport requirements
  • Generate e-Invoice & e-Way Bill - For invoices that need transport documentation

Based on your requirement you can generate e-invoices and e-way bills accordingly.
